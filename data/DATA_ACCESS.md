# Data Access

## MEPS 2008-2019
URL: https://meps.ahrq.gov/
Free and public. No registration required.

## NHANES 2011-2020
URL: https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/nhanes/
Free and public. No registration required.

## No IRB Required
All data de-identified per 45 CFR 46.104(d)(4)

## Variables Used
- Depression severity (PHQ-9)
- Family history bipolar disorder
- Healthcare utilization (ICD-10 codes)
- Psychotherapy sessions (CPT codes)
- Insurance coverage
- Geographic data (ZIP codes)
